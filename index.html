Dataset

What is this data about? Who produced this dataset? Why are you interested in it?

The dataset which I choose is "World Climate Change". The dataset contains information about the factors which affect climate change around the world and the steps countries have taken to reduce its effect.

This dataset is recorded and produced by the World Bank organization. It stresses to study the trends and patterns of what are main reasons for climate change? What quick steps need to take to reduce it and to save the earth.

Climate change is one of the most important issue the world is facing right now. Climate change is causing a wide range of impacts today. Extreme weather such as heatwaves, droughts, hurricanes and flooding rains and many more. These climate impacts threaten both the environment and our society. The climate change occurs due many factors increasing population, large amount of the c02 emission, destruction of the forest and agriculture land.
